About HTEP

Healthcare Text Extraction Platform

Our Mission

The Healthcare Text Extraction Platform (HTEP) simplifies the process of digitizing medical records. By leveraging advanced Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and AI, we transform locked PDF documents and images into editable, searchable text.

How It Works

  • Upload your medical report (PDF, PNG, or JPEG).
  • Our secure AI engine processes the image structure.
  • Receive clean, extracted text instantly.
